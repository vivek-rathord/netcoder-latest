Milestone 1 - Duration: 2 weeks

Digital Marketing, Websites & Landing Pages • 6 Live Sessions • 2 Projects • 2 Case Studies • 2 Cheat sheets • 2 Quizzes

This milestone builds the foundation of digital marketing by covering core concepts such as audience research, consumer behavior, branding, and funnel thinking. Learners will also create professional websites and conversion-focused landing pages using WordPress, aligning digital assets with real business and marketing objectives.

Week 1

Digital Marketing Foundation & AI Mindset

  • Digital marketing ecosystem & growth levers
  • Brand, performance & growth marketing models
  • Business models - D2C, B2B, EdTech, Local, SaaS, etc
  • Customer journey & funnel frameworks (TOFU-MOFU-BOFU)
  • Consumer behavior & buying triggers
  • ICPs, personas & audience research with AI
  • Market & competitor analysis using AI tools
  • Brand positioning, channels & KPI frameworks
Week 2

Website & Landing Page Design

  • Website fundamentals & digital marketing role
  • Business websites vs marketing websites
  • WordPress basics & dashboard navigation
  • Domain, hosting & website environment setup
  • Website structure & page hierarchy
  • WordPress configuration & theme customization
  • Pages, posts, menus & content management
  • Conversion-focused landing pages
  • User intent, buyer psychology & messaging
  • Headlines, CTAs, forms & trust elements
  • AI-assisted landing page ideation
  • No-code landing page design

Milestone 2 - Duration: 5 weeks

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) & Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) • 6 Live Sessions • 2 Projects • 2 Case Studies • 2 Cheat sheets • 2 Quizzes

This milestone focuses on building strong organic visibility through modern SEO and AEO practices. Learners will work on keyword strategy, on-page and technical SEO, content optimization, analytics, and live brand projects to understand how businesses drive long-term traffic and search dominance in the AI-driven search ecosystem.

Week 1

SEO Foundation & Keyword Research

  • How search engines work (Google, Bing, AI search)
  • SEO vs AEO - Understanding the shift
  • Keyword research strategies & tools (SEMrush, Ahrefs, Google Keyword Planner)
  • Long-tail keywords & search intent mapping
  • Competitor keyword analysis
  • Keyword clustering & topic modeling with AI
  • Search trends & seasonal optimization
Week 2

On-Page & Technical SEO

  • On-page optimization (title tags, meta descriptions, headers)
  • Content optimization & structured data (Schema markup)
  • URL structure & internal linking strategy
  • Technical SEO fundamentals (crawling, indexing, rendering)
  • XML sitemaps & robots.txt configuration
  • Core Web Vitals & page speed optimization
  • Mobile-first indexing & responsive design
  • Google Search Console & Bing Webmaster Tools setup

Milestone 3 - Duration: 4 weeks

Social Media, Content & Influencer Marketing • 12 Live Sessions • 4 Projects • 4 Case Studies • 4 Cheat sheets • 4 Quizzes

This milestone covers organic social media growth, content strategy, video marketing, influencer campaigns, and community-led branding. Learners will create platform-native content, work with creators, and design content-led growth systems to build reach, trust, and engagement across multiple digital platforms.

Week 1

Social Media Strategy & Content Planning

  • Social media landscape & platform selection
  • Creating a social media strategy aligned with business goals
  • Content pillars & content calendar creation
  • Platform-native content creation (Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter, YouTube)
  • Visual content design (Canva, Adobe Express)
  • Video content strategy (Reels, Shorts, TikTok)
  • User-generated content & community building
  • Social listening & engagement strategies
Week 2

Influencer Marketing & Campaign Management

  • Influencer marketing ecosystem (Micro, Macro, Mega influencers)
  • Identifying & vetting the right influencers
  • Influencer outreach & negotiation strategies
  • Campaign brief creation & content guidelines
  • Performance tracking & ROI measurement
  • Affiliate marketing fundamentals
  • Brand advocacy & community-led growth
  • Social media analytics & reporting tools

Milestone 4 - Duration: 4 weeks

Performance Marketing • 12 Live Sessions • 7 Projects • 6 Case Studies • 7 Cheat sheets • 7 Quizzes

This milestone is dedicated to paid marketing and growth optimization across Meta, Google Search, Display, YouTube, and e-commerce platforms. Learners will plan, execute, track, and optimize performance-driven campaigns using real tools, conversion tracking, and data-backed decision-making frameworks.

Week 1

Google Ads & Search Marketing

  • Google Ads ecosystem & account structure
  • Search campaign setup & keyword match types
  • Ad copywriting & extensions optimization
  • Bidding strategies & budget management
  • Quality score & ad rank optimization
  • Display & Discovery campaigns
  • YouTube advertising (in-stream, discovery, bumper ads)
  • Remarketing & audience targeting strategies
Week 2

Meta Ads & Conversion Optimization

  • Facebook & Instagram Ads Manager setup
  • Campaign objectives & ad formats (image, video, carousel, collection)
  • Audience targeting (core, custom, lookalike audiences)
  • Creative strategy & ad copy best practices
  • Pixel setup & conversion tracking
  • A/B testing & campaign optimization
  • Retargeting strategies & funnel optimization
  • Performance reporting & ROAS analysis

Milestone 5 - Duration: 2 weeks

Analytics, Reporting & Career Readiness • 6 Live Sessions • 2 Projects • 2 Case Studies • 2 Cheat sheets • 2 Quizzes

This milestone focuses on data-driven decision making, analytics implementation, and career preparation. Learners will master Google Analytics, data visualization, reporting frameworks, and complete their final portfolio project to showcase their expertise to potential employers.

Week 1

Analytics & Data Visualization

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4) setup & configuration
  • Event tracking & conversion goals
  • Audience analysis & user behavior insights
  • Data Studio / Looker Studio dashboard creation
  • Key metrics & KPIs for different channels
  • Attribution modeling & multi-channel funnels
  • Data storytelling & presentation skills
  • Marketing analytics tools (Supermetrics, Tableau)
Week 2

Career Readiness & Portfolio Finalization

  • Resume optimization for digital marketing roles
  • Portfolio presentation & case study documentation
  • LinkedIn profile optimization & personal branding
  • Mock interviews & interview preparation
  • Freelancing & agency setup guidance
  • Industry certifications (Google, Meta, HubSpot)
  • Final project presentation & feedback
  • Job placement assistance & networking
