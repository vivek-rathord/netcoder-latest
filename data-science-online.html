Milestone 1 - Duration: 2 weeks

Python, Statistics & Data Fundamentals • 6 Live Sessions • 2 Projects • 2 Case Studies

This milestone builds a strong foundation in Data Science and AI by introducing Python programming, statistics, and data handling concepts. Learners will understand how data is collected, processed, and analyzed to extract meaningful insights.

Week 1

Python Programming & Data Handling

  • Introduction to Python & environment setup
  • Variables, data types, loops & functions
  • Working with files & datasets
  • NumPy & Pandas basics
  • Data cleaning & preprocessing
Week 2

Statistics & Data Analysis

  • Descriptive statistics (mean, median, mode)
  • Probability fundamentals
  • Data distribution & visualization
  • Correlation & regression basics
  • Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

Milestone 2 - Duration: 5 weeks

Data Analysis, Visualization & SQL • 10 Live Sessions • 2 Projects

This milestone focuses on analyzing real-world datasets using SQL, Python, and visualization tools. Learners will transform raw data into meaningful insights and dashboards.

Week 1

SQL & Database Management

  • Introduction to databases & SQL
  • CRUD operations (SELECT, INSERT, UPDATE)
  • Joins, subqueries & aggregations
  • Data extraction for analysis
  • Working with large datasets
Week 2

Data Visualization & Dashboarding

  • Data visualization principles
  • Matplotlib & Seaborn
  • Power BI / Tableau basics
  • Interactive dashboards
  • Storytelling with data

Milestone 3 - Duration: 4 weeks

Machine Learning Fundamentals • 12 Live Sessions

This milestone introduces machine learning concepts including supervised and unsupervised learning. Learners will build models and understand how predictions and automation work.

Week 1

Supervised Learning & Regression

  • Introduction to machine learning
  • Linear & logistic regression
  • Model training & evaluation
  • Overfitting & underfitting
  • Performance metrics
Week 2

Unsupervised Learning & Clustering

  • K-Means clustering
  • Dimensionality reduction
  • Feature engineering
  • Model optimization
  • Real-world ML applications

Milestone 4 - Duration: 4 weeks

Artificial Intelligence & Deep Learning • 12 Live Sessions

This milestone focuses on AI concepts including neural networks, deep learning, and real-world AI applications such as NLP and computer vision.

Week 1

Deep Learning & Neural Networks

  • Introduction to neural networks
  • Activation functions & layers
  • TensorFlow / PyTorch basics
  • Model training & tuning
  • AI project implementation
Week 2

NLP & Computer Vision

  • Natural Language Processing basics
  • Text classification & sentiment analysis
  • Image processing fundamentals
  • Object detection basics
  • AI use cases & deployment

Milestone 5 - Duration: 2 weeks

Deployment, Projects & Career Readiness • 6 Live Sessions

This milestone focuses on deploying AI models, building portfolios, and preparing for careers in Data Science and AI roles.

Week 1

Model Deployment & MLOps

  • Model deployment basics
  • Flask / API integration
  • Cloud deployment (AWS basics)
  • Model monitoring & scaling
  • Real-world deployment case study
Week 2

Career Readiness & Portfolio

  • Resume building for Data Science roles
  • Portfolio projects & GitHub optimization
  • Mock interviews & coding rounds
  • LinkedIn optimization
  • Job assistance & networking

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our Data Science & AI training program, curriculum, and career opportunities.
Who can join Data Science & AI course?

This course is ideal for beginners, graduates, and working professionals who want to build a career in Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, and Machine Learning.

Do I need a strong math or coding background?

No, you don’t need advanced math or coding knowledge. We start from basics and gradually cover Python, statistics, and machine learning concepts in a simple way.

What technologies will I learn?

You will learn Python, NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, AI models, and tools like Jupyter Notebook and Scikit-learn.

Will I work on real-world data projects?

Yes, you will work on real datasets like sales prediction, fraud detection, recommendation systems, and AI-based classification projects.

Will I get a certificate after completion?

Yes, you will receive a recognized certificate after completing the course, which helps in job applications and internships.

Is there internship or placement support?

Yes, we provide internship opportunities, project-based learning, resume building, and placement assistance for eligible students.
