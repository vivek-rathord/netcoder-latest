Milestone 1 - Duration: 2 weeks

Digital Marketing, Websites & Landing Pages • 6 Live Sessions • 2 Projects • 2 Case Studies

This milestone builds the foundation of digital marketing by covering core concepts such as audience research, consumer behavior, branding, and funnel thinking.

Week 1

Digital Marketing Foundation & AI Mindset

  • Digital marketing ecosystem & growth levers
  • Brand, performance & growth marketing models
  • Customer journey & funnel frameworks
  • Consumer behavior & buying triggers
  • ICPs, personas & audience research with AI
Week 2

Website & Landing Page Design

  • Website fundamentals & digital marketing role
  • WordPress basics & dashboard navigation
  • Domain, hosting & website environment setup
  • Conversion-focused landing pages
  • User intent, buyer psychology & messaging
PROJECT

Brand & Digital Marketing Strategy for a Local Business

Collaborate with a local restaurant or café to analyze customers, competitors, and brand goals. Build a marketing brief, define brand positioning, and design an AI-powered strategy.

Website & Landing Page Setup for a Business

Build a website and landing page for a business. Configure CMS, design pages, and create a conversion-focused experience.

Swiggy Instamart

Analyze how quick-commerce platforms scale using customer insights and fast delivery models.

Nykaa

Understand how structured content and user journeys help convert visitors into customers.

Milestone 2 - Duration: 5 weeks

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) & Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) • 6 Live Sessions • 2 Projects

This milestone focuses on building strong organic visibility through modern SEO and AEO practices. Learners will work on keyword strategy, on-page and technical SEO, and content optimization.

Week 1

SEO Foundation & Keyword Research

  • How search engines work (Google, Bing, AI search)
  • SEO vs AEO - Understanding the shift
  • Keyword research strategies & tools
  • Long-tail keywords & search intent mapping
  • Keyword clustering & topic modeling with AI
Week 2

On-Page & Technical SEO

  • On-page optimization (title tags, meta descriptions)
  • Content optimization & structured data
  • Technical SEO fundamentals (crawling, indexing)
  • XML sitemaps & robots.txt configuration
  • Core Web Vitals & page speed optimization

Milestone 3 - Duration: 4 weeks

Social Media, Content & Influencer Marketing • 12 Live Sessions

This milestone covers organic social media growth, content strategy, video marketing, influencer campaigns, and community-led branding.

Week 1

Social Media Strategy & Content Planning

  • Social media landscape & platform selection
  • Creating a social media strategy
  • Content pillars & content calendar creation
  • Platform-native content creation
  • Video content strategy (Reels, Shorts)
Week 2

Influencer Marketing & Campaign Management

  • Influencer marketing ecosystem
  • Identifying & vetting the right influencers
  • Campaign brief creation & content guidelines
  • Performance tracking & ROI measurement
  • Affiliate marketing fundamentals

Milestone 4 - Duration: 4 weeks

Performance Marketing • 12 Live Sessions

This milestone is dedicated to paid marketing and growth optimization across Meta, Google Search, Display, YouTube, and e-commerce platforms.

Week 1

Google Ads & Search Marketing

  • Google Ads ecosystem & account structure
  • Search campaign setup & keyword match types
  • Ad copywriting & extensions optimization
  • Bidding strategies & budget management
  • Display & Discovery campaigns
Week 2

Meta Ads & Conversion Optimization

  • Facebook & Instagram Ads Manager setup
  • Campaign objectives & ad formats
  • Audience targeting (core, custom, lookalike)
  • Pixel setup & conversion tracking
  • A/B testing & campaign optimization

Milestone 5 - Duration: 2 weeks

Analytics, Reporting & Career Readiness • 6 Live Sessions

This milestone focuses on data-driven decision making, analytics implementation, and career preparation. Learners will master Google Analytics and complete their final portfolio project.

Week 1

Analytics & Data Visualization

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4) setup & configuration
  • Event tracking & conversion goals
  • Audience analysis & user behavior insights
  • Data Studio / Looker Studio dashboard creation
  • Attribution modeling & multi-channel funnels
Week 2

Career Readiness & Portfolio Finalization

  • Resume optimization for digital marketing roles
  • Portfolio presentation & case study documentation
  • LinkedIn profile optimization & personal branding
  • Mock interviews & interview preparation
  • Job placement assistance & networking
